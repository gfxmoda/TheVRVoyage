How it works

Skip the flights, the layover, the jetlag, and forget about travel restrictions with The VR Voyage. Our virtual experiences are bringing the world to you. Not sure how it works? Learn our process a bit better below.

1. Choose your perfect tour

Search our site by destination or by interest and find the perfect virtual experience for you. Whether you want to explore a new city, investigate a particular period in history, or experience nature at its finest, there is something right for you. Add that tour to your cart.

2. Purchase your tour

Follow the instructions to complete your purchase. This is the time when you would input any promotional codes or Partner codes you may have.

3. Create your account

In order to access your virtual tour, you will need to create an account through our site. This is where you will find each tour you purchase. No billing information will be kept in relation to this account.

4. Email Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation about your tour. It will now be active on your account and ready for you to start exploring.

5. Take your tour

Your tour will be on your account for 48 hours after you activate the tour. After that period the tour link on your account will expire.
